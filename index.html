Featured Project


Water wave generator machine

The following project is one of the most recent collaborations. The project's aim as its name suggest is the generation of water waves for recreational purposes in an amusement park in Ecuador.

The project is a mechanical design of a machine with enougth power to move water and generate a wave for recreational purposes. It has a lot of physical considerations, previous knowledge and mathematical develop. The image of the project is from an early prototype and due to owner patent rigths can not be shown the actual shape of the machine.
